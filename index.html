ABOUT US

We Make The Best Collection Of Wedding Dresses for Women

Every bride wants to look beautiful on her wedding day and the best way to look good is to feel good. With over 14 years’ of experience in designing wedding dresses, we understand this very well. We provide the kind of personal service brides need to look and feel their best on their wedding day.

At ElanDesignStudio, each gown is designed and manufactured with exquisite hand detailing for exceptional quality and incredible value. To complement our gowns, we offer customized changes for today's bride to create a one of a kind gown to make her dreams come true.

Our specialty is Couture made to measure, We also design and make the entire bridal ensemble, which includes dresses for bridesmaids, flower girls and mothers of the brides. Apart f rom weddings, we even specialize in evening gowns, cocktail dresses, party and other dresses
